Teaching & Mentorship

I view teaching as the cultivation of independent mathematical thinking. My courses aim to bridge rigorous theory in information and coding theory with engineering intuition, helping students build the mathematical foundations needed for research and professional work. I teach at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, and have also taught at the University of Maryland.
